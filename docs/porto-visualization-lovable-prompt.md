# Why Porto? - Visualization for Lovable

> **[DIAGRAM PROMPT FOR LOVABLE]**
>
> Create a single-page split-screen infographic comparing Traditional Wallets vs Porto. This is a presentation slide, not an app. Use a 16:9 aspect ratio layout.
>
> **Header:**
> - Title: "Why Porto?" in large bold text, centered
> - Subtitle: "The future of wallet UX" in smaller muted text below
>
> **Layout:** Two columns side by side with a thin vertical divider in the center.
>
> **Left Column - "Traditional Wallet":**
> - Column header: "Traditional Wallet" with a red/orange accent
> - Background: darker, heavier feel with subtle red tint
> - Show 6 numbered steps as a vertical list, each with a small icon:
>   1. Install browser extension (puzzle piece icon)
>   2. Write down 12-word seed phrase (pencil/paper icon)
>   3. Buy ETH on an exchange (dollar/coin icon)
>   4. Bridge ETH to the right chain (bridge/arrow icon)
>   5. Approve token - popup #1 (window/alert icon)
>   6. Execute action - popup #2 (window/alert icon)
> - At the bottom, a warning callout in red: "Lost seed phrase = Lost funds forever"
> - Overall feel: cluttered, many steps, friction
>
> **Right Column - "Porto" (smooth/simple):**
> - Column header: "Porto" with a green/teal accent
> - Background: cleaner, lighter feel with subtle green/blue tint
> - Show just 2 steps prominently:
>   1. Tap fingerprint / Face ID (fingerprint icon)
>   2. Done. You're in. (checkmark icon)
> - Below, show 4 bonus benefits as small pill badges or icon+text pairs:
>   - "Gas sponsored" (gas pump icon with slash)
>   - "One-click batching" (layers/stack icon)
>   - "Account recovery" (shield/heart icon)
>   - "No extension needed" (globe/browser icon)
> - At the bottom, a positive callout in green: "Same address on every chain"
> - Overall feel: clean, minimal, effortless
>
> **Footer:**
> - Centered text: "AgoRace - Powered by Porto (EIP-7702)"
>
> **Style:**
> - Dark theme with subtle gradients (dark navy/charcoal background)
> - Left side: red/orange accents for pain points, slightly cluttered feel
> - Right side: green/teal/blue accents for benefits, clean and spacious
> - Modern sans-serif font
> - Small, simple icons (line-art style, not filled)
> - Presentation-friendly: high contrast text, readable from a distance
> - No interactive elements - this is a static visual/slide
